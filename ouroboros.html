Ouroboros Engine

Robust and powerful 3D Engine for Dynamic Creations.

The Full-fledged Ouroboros Engine was developed by a passionate team of eight tech students, emphasizing usability and performance. It is a complete rebuild from the previous Ouroboros2D Engine, with a focus on delivering a seamless user experience.
